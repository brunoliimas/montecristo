Welcome to Lister Horsfall

Official Rolex Retailer

Lister Horsfall of Halifax and Ilkley are proud to be part of the worldwide network of official Rolex Retailers, allowed to sell and maintain Rolex watches. With the necessary skills, technical know-how and special equipment, we guarantee the authenticity of each and every part of your Rolex. Browse the Rolex collection below, or simply contact us to book an appointment with our dedicated staff, who can help you make the choice that will last a lifetime.
